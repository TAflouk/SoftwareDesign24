Terms

ESharing allows you to rent cars for as little as an hour, using a booking system to reserve the vehicle and in-car technology to unlock the car and drive. We have over 700 cars & vans available across Ireland, mostly within Dublin City.

A ESharing for Business account provides companies with the freedom and flexibility of owning a large fleet without the capital investment. There are no monthly fees so you will only be charged for what you use. The process is simple

With over 700 vehicles across the country we can get you where you need to go. Basic insurance included, additional cover (€250 excess) available for only €2.40 per hour/€100 annual fee. Fuel is included, each car has a Circle K fuel card inside.
* EACH TRIP INCLUDES 50KM FREE, KILOMETERS OVER 50 CHARGED AT €0.50 PER KM. GOFAR LONG DISTANCE JOURNEYS €0.15 PER KM AVAILABLE FOR BOOKINGS OVER 6 HOURS.

We ask that you return the car with at least a quarter tank of fuel.
